## Scopes

These two components are identical and share the same purpose. Using `Scopes` keeps your codebase updated in case Spotify changes the names of one of the scopes, and helps prevent mistypes.

<Playground>
  <SpotifyAuth
    scopes={[
      Scopes.userFollowRead,
      Scopes.userFollowModify,
      Scopes.userReadRecentlyPlayed
    ]}
  />

  <br />

  <SpotifyAuth
    scopes={[
      'user-follow-read',
      'user-follow-modify',
      'user-read-recently-played'
    ]}
  />
</Playground>
